* What is Bulgogi?

It's Korean-style marinated beef in a sweet and savory sauce
made with soy sauce sugar, garlic and onion.

How to Make

?

CHECK LIST

WATER

ONE POT

COOKING DIRECTION

PUT PASTA NOODLES AND FLAKES
INTO 1 1/3 CUPS [ 10.65 FL OZ (315mL)] OF

30 SECONDS, STIRRING FREQUENTLY.
