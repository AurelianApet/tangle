KIMCHI ROSÉ
TANGLUCCINE

3.88 OZ (110g)

KOREAN-INSPIRED FETTUCCINE
WITH KIMCHI IN CREAMY TOMATO SAUCE

* What is Kimchi?

It's a traditional Korean side dish made of napa cabbage
with spices such as red pepper, garlic, and ginger

How to Make

?

CHECK LIST

1 BAG

WATER

ONE POT

COOKING DIRECTION

PUT PASTA NOODLES AND FLAKES
INTO 1 1/3 CUPS [ 10.65 FL OZ (315mL)] OF
BOILING WATER AND COOK FOR 5 MINUTRES
30 SECONDS, STIRRING FREQUENTLY.

REMOVE FROM HEAT,
AND THEN ADD SAUCE AND POWDER.

STIR WELL AND SERVE YOUR TANGLE!

Cooking Time TIP!

41⁄2 min

51⁄2 min
