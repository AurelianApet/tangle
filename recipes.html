BULGOGI ALFREDO STEAK TANGLUCCINE

Restaurant-quality dining from the comfort of your home with tangle bulgogi alfredo tangluccine

Ingredients

⋄ Water 10.65 FL OZ (315ml)
⋄ Tangle bulgogi alfredo tangluccine 1 ea
⋄ Steak 2.8 oz
⋄ Salt and pepper
⋄ Mushroom 1/2 cup
⋄ Sliced garlic 1/4 cup
⋄ Olive oil 3 Tbsp
⋄ Rosemary

Time

09:00

Directions

1
Season the steak with salt and pepper.
2
In a pan over medium-high heat, heat the olive oil until shimmering. Add the seasoned steak, sliced garlic and mushrooms and cook about 3 minutes. Remove the steak from the pan and set aside.
3
Cook the tangle bulgogi alfredo tangluccine in the same pan according to package directions
4
Serve hot, garnished with rosemary.

BULGOGI ALFREDO SALAD TANGLUCCINE

Turn leftover ingredients into delicious meals at home with tangle bulgogi alfredo tangluccine

Ingredients

⋄ Water 10.65 FL OZ (315ml)
⋄ Tangle bulgogi alfredo tangluccine 1 ea
⋄ Vegetables for salad
⋄ Oriental dressing 2 Tbsp

Time

08:00

Directions

1
Cut vegetables into bite-size pieces.
2
After boiling the noodles 5 minute and 30 seconds, rinse them coldly and mix them with bulgogi sauce.
3
Put the vegetables in a bowl and sprinke the cream powder.
4
Add oriental dressing and mix it.

KIMCHI ROSÉ OVEN TANGLUCCINE

Turn leftover ingredients into delicious meals at home with tangle kimchi rosé tangluccine

Ingredients

⋄ Water 10.65 FL OZ (315ml)
⋄ Tangle kimchi rosé tangluccine 1 ea
⋄ Sausage 1 ea
⋄ Broccoli 1/4 cup
⋄ Parika 1 ea
⋄ Onion 1/4 ea
⋄ Mozzarella cheese 4 Tbsp
⋄ Parmesan cheese 1 Tbsp
⋄ Parsley 1 tbsp

Time

10:00

Directions

1
Slice the sausage, broccoli, parika, and onion into small pieces.
2
Cook the tangle kimchi rose tangluccine adding the sausage and broccoli.
3
After cooking, place mozzarella cheese and paprika on pasta and bake them in the oven in 3 minutes.
4
Put parmesan cheese and parsley as toppings.
