KOREAN-INSPIRED FETTUCCINE

* What is Kimchi?

It's a traditional Korean side dish
made of napa cabbage
with spices such as red pepper,
garlic, and ginger

How to Make

?

CHECK LIST

1 BAG

WATER

ONE POT

COOKING DIRECTION

PUT PASTA NOODLES AND FLAKES
INTO 1 1/3 CUPS [ 10.65 FL OZ (315mL)] OF
BOILING WATER AND COOK FOR 5 MINUTRES
30 SECONDS, STIRRING FREQUENTLY.

REMOVE FROM HEAT,
AND THEN ADD SAUCE AND POWDER.

STIR WELL AND SERVE YOUR TANGLE!
