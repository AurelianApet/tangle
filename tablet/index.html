STORY!

'Tangle' is Korean-inspired pasta, also know as K-pasta,

made with air-dried noodles and Korean-style signature sauce.

The name 'Tangle' comes from the Korean word(탱글)

for a firm and elastic texture, which perfectly describes

our firm and bouncy noodles.

K-pasta is a new twist on a classic dish, reinterpreted in Korean style.

Our super easy one-pot pasta recipe is perfect for every day.

Make your life lively with Tangle.
